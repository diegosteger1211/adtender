# ADR-002 Knowledge before Documents

## Decision

Documents are not the primary source of truth.

Structured Business Objects and Knowledge Assets are the primary source of truth.

## Rationale

Documents cannot support reliable comparison, evaluation, traceability and AI analysis at enterprise quality.

## Consequences

Documents may be generated, attached, imported or exported, but must not replace structured platform data.
