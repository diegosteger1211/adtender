# Product DNA

## Purpose

This document defines the identity of adtender.

It is the constitutional document of the platform and is valid for product management, architecture, development and AI-assisted implementation.

## Vision

adtender helps organizations plan, execute, evaluate and document complex tender and decision processes.

The long-term vision is an Enterprise Decision & Knowledge Platform where every completed project improves future projects.

## Mission

adtender supports organizations in making structured, transparent and reusable business decisions.

## Product Philosophy

### Decisions instead of Documents

Documents are views, exports, attachments or evidence.

The primary value is structured decision knowledge.

### Knowledge instead of Archives

Completed projects are not simply archived.

They improve the organizational knowledge base.

### Reuse instead of Recreation

Existing knowledge should be reused before new content is created.

### Platform instead of One-Off Application

Tender Management is the first use case.

The architecture must remain open for future decision-centric domains.

### Human Responsibility

AI supports users, but accountable decisions remain with humans.

## Business Scope

adtender supports:

- Project initiation
- Requirement engineering
- Tender creation
- Supplier collaboration
- Structured supplier responses
- Evaluation
- Decision documentation
- Award and handover
- Lessons learned
- Knowledge reuse

## Out of Scope

adtender does not replace:

- ERP
- CRM
- DMS
- Accounting
- HR
- Full Contract Lifecycle Management

adtender integrates with such systems.
