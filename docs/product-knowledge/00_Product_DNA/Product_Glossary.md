# Product Glossary

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| Project | Business initiative that frames a tender or decision process |
| Requirement | Reusable business requirement or expectation |
| Requirement Library | Collection of reusable requirements |
| Tender | Structured procurement or selection process |
| Supplier | External organization participating in a tender |
| Tender Response | Structured supplier answer to tender requirements |
| Evaluation | Structured assessment of supplier responses |
| Decision | Accountable business decision with rationale |
| Knowledge Asset | Reusable organizational knowledge |
| Workflow | Configurable orchestration of business tasks |
| Business Object | Domain concept with identity, lifecycle and relationships |
| Audit Record | Immutable record of a relevant business action |
| Version | Immutable snapshot of a business object state |
