# Product Principles

## P-001 Business Objects First

Business Objects define the platform. Screens, APIs and database structures are derived from them.

## P-002 Knowledge by Design

Every function should contribute to structured knowledge or decision quality.

## P-003 Reuse by Default

Before creating new content, the platform should support reuse of existing knowledge.

## P-004 Configuration before Programming

Business behavior should be configurable whenever reasonable.

## P-005 Human in the Loop

AI and automation support users but do not replace accountable decisions.

## P-006 Explainability

All recommendations, evaluations and decisions must be explainable.

## P-007 Traceability

Important business information must remain traceable across its lifecycle.

## P-008 Domain Neutrality

The platform must not be hardcoded for one industry or security domain.

## P-009 Platform Thinking

New capabilities must be reusable and extensible.

## P-010 Decision Documentation

The path to a decision is as important as the decision itself.
