# adtender Product Knowledge Base

This repository area is the Single Source of Truth for the development of the adtender platform.

## Purpose

The Product Knowledge Base contains the business, architectural and implementation knowledge required to develop adtender consistently.

It is written for:

- Product Management
- Business Architecture
- Software Architecture
- Developers
- UX Designers
- QA Engineers
- AI Development Agents

## Product Identity

adtender is an Enterprise Decision & Knowledge Platform.

Tender Management is the first business capability implemented on the platform.

## Repository Structure

- `00_Product_DNA` – product identity, principles, architecture rules and ADRs
- `01_Business` – business processes, capabilities and business domains
- `02_Domain_Model` – business objects, aggregates and relationships
- `03_Functional` – functional capability specifications
- `04_UI` – workspaces, navigation and user journeys
- `05_Technical` – services, APIs, events and architecture
- `06_AI` – AI guidelines, prompting and decision intelligence
- `07_Development` – coding guidelines and implementation rules

## Mandatory Rule

No implementation starts before the related business object and business capability are described.
